Who We Are

Through a blend of innovation, expertise, and a client‑focused approach, FAS Services empowers industries to meet today’s challenges and build tomorrow’s success stories. We deliver excellence through smart engineering and reliable energy solutions—offering not just products and services, but long‑term partnerships built on integrity, innovation, and performance.

Vision

To be a regional leader in engineering‑driven industrial solutions, recognized for innovation, reliability, and the ability to power progress.

Mission

To deliver engineering‑driven solutions by leveraging deep technical expertise, strategic partnerships, and the highest standards of safety and quality.

Business Activities

We operate across oil & gas, petrochemicals, mining, power generation, mineral processing, metals, renewables, waste recycling, and construction (residential, commercial, industrial). Services include engineering support & management, design oversight, inspection, and supplier/contractor audits.

Global Presence

Collaboration & engineering offices in: USA, UK, Italy, France, India, China, Turkey and GCC: Saudi Arabia (Dammam • Riyadh • Yanbu), Dubai, Kuwait.
